Our roots

About DCW Grows

DCW Grows is a Southern California plant shop and gardening resource created by plant lovers who believe growing should feel fun, practical, and approachable.

What We Grow

Our shop features healthy live plants, rooted starts, succulents, cactus, outdoor favorites, and seasonal garden finds. Our selection changes as our own garden grows.

More Than a Plant Shop

We share real growing lessons, detailed plant guides, planting tips, and friendly help through our Garden Wizard. We want customers to feel supported after the box arrives—not left alone with a mystery plant and a tiny care tag.

Grown Differently

DCW Grows keeps the personality in gardening. We test, learn, laugh at the chaos, and share what actually works in a real home garden.
